Supper

Activity

1. In your notebook, make a list of the things you ate the last three days, separate the food depending on the number of meals you ate during each day, specify the name of the meal according to what you learned.

Example: day 1

Breakfast: milk, and donut

Lunch: chicken and rice

2. Then classify the food into the differents groups that you already learned. 

Grains and cereals Fruits and vegetables Meat Milk and alternatives Fats and sugar

3. Finally, analyze your list carefully and answer the questions according to the food pyramid:

  • Do you respect the portions that your body needs? If you answer yes to this question, good for you, if not, think about the changes you need to do to eat better.
  • How healthy do you eat?
