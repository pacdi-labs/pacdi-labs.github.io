Elevenses

Reading Activity

 Read the information 

You are probably asking yourself,  what is a meal?          

Well, a meal is an act or the time of eating a portion of food to satisfy your appetite. The number of meals a person eats during the day depends on every country's customs and its history. Most people probably eat about three main meals every day, but here are seven words for meals that we often use: 

  • breakfast
    The first meal of the day. Usually around 6 a.m.- 9 p.m.

  • brunch
    A meal that people eat in the late morning, instead of breakfast and lunch. 

  • elevenses
    A light snack and drink (very popular meal in the United Kingdom), people eat it in the late morning after breakfast and before lunch around 11 a.m., for example, biscuits and coffee. 

  • lunch
    A meal in the middle of the day. Usually around noon or 1 p.m.

  • tea
    A light afternoon meal of sandwiches, cakes etc, with a drink of tea taken around 4 p.m. Another name for it is: "afternoon tea" (mainly in the United Kingdom). The word tea can also refer to a cooked evening meal, around 6 p.m. 

  • supper
    A light or informal evening meal. Around 6 p.m. - 7 p.m.

  • dinner
    For some cultures it can be the most important meal of the day. Depending on the culture, people eat it in the middle of the day or in the evening. Usually when people say "dinner", they mean an evening meal, around 7 p.m. - 9 p.m.

True-False Question

Based on the information in the previous text, read sentences 1-5 and decide if the information is True or False.

Question 1

1. Is the elevenses meal around 6:00 am?

Feedback

False

The elevenses meal is around 11:00 a.m.

Question 2

2. Does the number of meals depend on the country's history?

Feedback

True

 A person eats a certain number of meals depending on his/her culture and history.

Question 3

3. Do most people probably eat about five main meals every day?

Feedback

False

Most people eat three meals per day.

Question 4

4. Is the supper meal more important than dinner?

Feedback

False

Dinner is the main meal of the day.

Question 5

5.  A meal is the time of eating a portion of food.

Feedback

True

A meal is a regular occasion in a day when you eat.
