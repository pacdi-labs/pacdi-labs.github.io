Read the following information about natural disasters. Pay close attention to the descriptions to answer the activities. You can take notes in your notebook to review the vocabulary. Remember that if you do not understand something, you can look up the words in the dictionary.  

NATURAL DISASTERS

Our planet can be a calm place, but weather conditions often change rapidly. The weather can be very powerful. A natural disaster is a natural event that creates dangerous and destructive conditions. It is important to learn about natural disasters so you can be prepared if you face one. There are several kinds of natural disasters that may occur around the world every year. Each year, the Earth experiences natural disasters. When natural disasters occur in populated areas, a lot of people  lose their lives. Sometimes, one disaster is related to another. For example: volcanic eruptions can cause earthquakes, and earthquakes can cause landslides and tsunamis. We cannot prevent natural disasters entirely.
