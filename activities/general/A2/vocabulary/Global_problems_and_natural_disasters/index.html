What is happening around your city?

 

Most common problems that affect your city! 

All cities around the world have their own problems, in some places some of the problems might be more severe than in other places. Some cities might consider traffic a big problem. In other places, people are not responsible with their trash, they throw it on the ground and this causes litter on the streets.  There are other places full of factories that produce pollution. 

Answer these questions in your notebook.

1. What type of problems are common in your area?

2. Do these problems affect all the community? 

3. Are these problems severe? 

4. Who or what  do you think caused these problems?

5. Do these problems have a solution? 

In this lesson, we will talk about some of the most common problems that affect our lives every day.

If you want to know more about this topic, continue working with this material.
