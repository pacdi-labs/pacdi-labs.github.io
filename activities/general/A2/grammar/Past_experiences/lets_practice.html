Let's practice!

Ben's story

Usually when we go on vacations, we expect everything to go well, but sometimes this is not the case. Read Ben's story about his last holiday. There are 8 mistakes in the verbs, find them to correct them. Once you find them write the correct sentences in your notebook then click on "Show feedback" to compare your answers. 

Ben's vacation

Martin had a terrible day yesterday. It was the day of his vacation to Spain but it was a nightmare. He leave the house at 10 o'clock in the morning and catched a taxi to the airport.

Martin went to the check-in with his luggage. When the man who worked for the airline asked for his passport, Martin became very upset. Where was his passport? Martin realize he didn't had his passport with him. What a disaster!

He jumped back into the taxi and told the driver to take him home. The driver drived duickly and Martin arrived back at his house in twenty minutes.

He runned into the house and goed into his bedroom. He found his passport on the bedside table and left the house again. But the taxi be not there! Where was the taxi!

Martin jumped up and down angrily. "I don't believe it. The taxi left!" he shouted. He ran down the street and looked for another taxi.

After a few minutes, Martin found another taxi and told the driver to take him to the airport as quickly as possible. The traffic on the roads was very bad and, unfortunately, Martin's taxi arrived at the airport late. His plane left for Spain at 11.30 and Martin missed it. He was very sad and went to speak to the airline. They promised to get him a seat on an evening flight and Martin went home on another taxi to wait.

Feedback

The correct sentences are: 

1. He left the house at 10 o'clock in the morning and caught a taxi to the airport.

2. Martin realized he didn't have his passport with him.

3. The driver drove quickly.

4. He ran into the house and went into his bedroom.

5. But the taxi wasn't there!

Bad experiences?

Have you ever had a bad experience travelling? What went wrong?

It doesn't have to be negative, you can write about your best travel experiences.  

Write a short paragraph telling your experience. Remember that you need to use the simple past to narrate events in the past. 

Be careful with the use of regular and irregular verbs.
