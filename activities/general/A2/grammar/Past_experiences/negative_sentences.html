Negative sentences

How to form negative sentences in simple past?

We need an auxiliary verb to form sentences in simple past: did

Structure: subject + negative auxiliary (did not) + base form of the verb + rest of the sentence

Do not conjugate the main verb after did not (didn't).

More examples: 

  • He did not visit his grandmother. 
  • We did not play soccer last week.
  • They did not see the movie yesterday.
