Objective

What will you learn?

  • In this material you will learn about the uses of simple past, and you will complete some activities about this topic to evaluate what you learned. 
  • You will also learn the structures of affirmative, negative and interrogative sentences in simple past to help you express events your own experiences in the past.
  • You will recognize and identify some regular and irregular verbs through traveling experiences.
