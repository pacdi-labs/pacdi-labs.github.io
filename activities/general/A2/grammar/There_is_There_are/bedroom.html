Bedroom

The bedroom

And finally... my bedroom!

Look at the picture and the following questions.

Is there a bed?

Yes, there is.

Are there any pillows on the bed?

Yes, there are.

Is there a cookie next to the coffee?

No, there isn't.

Are there any family photographs?

No, there aren't.

Questions

We form questions using is / are in front of there (the opposite position) 

We use any with plural questions and uncountable nouns.

  • Is there any music in the bedroom? - music is an uncountable noun.
  • Are there any jeans in the closet? - jeans is a plural noun.

We use there is / are in short answers.

  • Is there a dog in the bedroom? - No, there isn't.
  • Are there any dogs in the bedroom? - No, there aren't.
  • Is there an air-conditioner? - No, there isn't.
  • Are there any lights in the bedroom? - Yes, there are.
