What about your house?

Closing

The tour around my house is finished!

And now you can use correctly there is and there are.

  • Write 6 sentences describing the objects that EXIST or DO NOT EXIST in your own house, for example:

There are two bedrooms in my house./There isn't a garden in my house.

Check your sentences:

1. Be sure that you used THERE IS/ISN'T or THERE ARE/AREN'T

2. Check the plural nouns with ARE and singulars with IS.

3. Use a dictionary to check your vocabulary and ortography.

  • Did you like this activity? In your notebook, write a short reflection about this activity.

I liked... I learned... It was difficult to...

  • Create a glossary with all the new words you learned in this activity.
