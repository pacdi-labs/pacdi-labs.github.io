What's wrong?

 

If you look at the images, can you recognize at first sight what the problem is? 

Do you think there is a solution to each problem? How would you solve each problem?

When you go into a hospital, you might find lots of different people, people with different health problems. You will meet lots of people there; from the moment you arrive until you’re ready to leave.

You might meet as many as 30 people or more just in one day! You’ll see plenty of doctors and nurses too.

People go to the hospital for many reasons. It may seem a little scary to go to a hospital, but doctors, nurses, and other hospital workers are there to help people who are sick to feel better.
