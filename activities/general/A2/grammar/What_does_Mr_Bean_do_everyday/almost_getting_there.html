What did I learn today?


According to the information and exercises that you have reviewed today. With five complete ideas,  write in your notebook the most important rules or recomendations to pay attention every time that you use Simple Present Tense. Then click  the bottom "Ideas to include" to see our sugestion.

Feedback

1. We use Simple Present to talk about routines.( the things that we repeat in a random way).

2. We use the auxiliaries do (we, you, they and I) and does (he, she, it) in questions and negative sentences.

3. We never use the auxiliary in affirmative sentences; then, we must add "s" to the verbs with the third person (He, she it).

4. We use some adverbs of frequency and time expressions as:

Adverbs: always, never, usually , often sometimes, seldom, rarerly, hardly ever.

Expressions o time: once (a month, a week a year.) every day.

5. We must respect the order of each element of Speech: QW +Aux+ S+ V +C ?
