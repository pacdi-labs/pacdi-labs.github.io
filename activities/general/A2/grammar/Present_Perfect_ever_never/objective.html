Objective

What am I learning?

In this lesson you will learn how to talk about the things you have done in your life with the Present Perfect. Also, you will practice this tense with the adverbs ever and never.

By the end of the lesson you will be able to express: 

  • Affirmative sentences
  • Negative sentences
  • Yes/No Questions

All in Present Perfect Simple!
