What did I learn?

Key points

Remember the following key points. Order them so they follow this structure: 1. Use of the Present Perfect 2.Time that the Present Perfect refers to 3. Structure 4. Negation 5. Questions

  • We use the Present Perfect to talk about past experiences. 
  • The exact time of the action is not important.
  • We form the Present Perfect with the auxiliary "have" in present and the main verb is in past participle.
  • Besides "not" we can use "never" in affirmative sentences. It means that we have not done something in our life.
  • We can add "ever" that means at some point in our life.
