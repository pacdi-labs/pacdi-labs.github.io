Spongebob is a generous creature who lives in Bikini Bottom. He works at the Krusty Krab cooking delicious hamburgers. His ambitious boss is named Mr. Krabs and his angry co-worker is named Squidward. Spongebob loves his job but many times has to confront difficult situations caused by their determined enemy: Plankton. In this episode, Spongebob discovers that Plankton may not be evil, just lonely.
