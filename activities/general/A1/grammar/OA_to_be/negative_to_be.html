Negative TO BE

 

 The verb to be has a negative form. 

I am not I'm not
You  are not You aren't
He is not He isn't
She is not She isn't
It is not It isn't
We are not We aren't
You are not You aren't
They are not They aren't

Read the examples next to the pictures.

 It is not a school. It is a house. 

 Bart is not happy. He is sad.

 They are not working. They are playing Monopoly. 

 You are not girls.  

 My friends are not in class. 

 I am not playing. I am reading a book.
