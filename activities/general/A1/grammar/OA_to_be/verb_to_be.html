Verb TO BE

 

We use the verb TO BE to say something about a person, thing, animal or to describe their state.

Look at the present conjugation of the verb TO BE:

I am
You  are
He is
She is
It  is
We  are
You are
They are

Look at the same conjugation in present with contractions. We form a contraction by combining two words:

I 'm I'm
You 're You're
He 's He's
She 's She's
It 's It's
We 're We're
You 're You're
They 're They're
