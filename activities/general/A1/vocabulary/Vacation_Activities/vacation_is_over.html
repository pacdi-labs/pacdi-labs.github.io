Vacation is over

 

 Vacation is over!

 It's time to go back to school 

Now you are ready to use this vocabuary.

Let's practice what you learned today. 

  • Write a short text describing your last vacation or your favorite vacation from your childhood.
  • Check that you use the correct past form of the verbs.
  • Use the vocabulary that you learned today.
  • If you don't know how to say a word, use the online dictionary.

Did you like this activity?

  • Write a short personal reflection in your notebook about this activity. Use sentences similar to:

I liked that... I learned that... It is difficult to... The activity is funny/boring/interesting...

  • Create a glossary with all the new words you learned in this activity.
