Common Sentences & Common Mistakes

Reading Activity

Now let's see how to express what we did on vacation.

Pay attention to the following rules.

How was your vacation?

  • For a period, use vacation (singular) - vacationS is wrong-.

I always go on vacation in July. 

  • Use ON with vacation -  IN vacation is wrong-.

For questions, use:

  • Where to mention the place. 

Where did you go? -I went to Chiapas.

  • When to mention the date or period. 

When did you visit London? - I visited London last year.

  • Who to say the people you were with. 

Who did you go with? -I went with my family.
