Winter Holidays

What can you do in winter?

  Winter is the coldest season of the year.

  Schools and universities have a winter break (or winter     holidays).

 Generally, holidays are the days to celebrate or commemorate a tradition of cultural or religious significance.

    In winters, there is usually snow and ice , so you can do this activities outside:

    • go snowboarding
    • ice skating
    • go skiing
    • make a snowman

    But, what if it is too cold outside and you want to stay warm at home? These are some indoor activities that you probably do in winter.

    • watching a movie
    • a TV series marathon
    • reading a book
    • playing board games
    • playing videogames
