Summer Activities

Summer vacations are the best!

Summer is traditionally associated with hot weather  and rain . 

Schools and universities usually have a summer break (or summer vacation).

People like to do outdoor activities, for example going to the beach, playing sports or camping. Also it is a good oportunity for students to work and earn some money. 

Let's see the most common summer activities.

  • camping
  • kayaking
  • sightseeing
  • snorkeling
  • sunbathing
  • swimming
  • boating
  • hiking
  • fishing
  • traveling
  • barbecuing
  • playing sports
  • bicycling
  • picnicking
  • working
