Previous Knowledge

 

This is a basic level activity, so don't worry if you don't recognize some words; you will learn them during the process. 

However, you need to know one important verb:

TO WEAR

"To Wear" = "Have on the body"

We don't say "use" when we refer to clothes.

  • Angelina Jolie is using a black dress. 
  • Angelina Jolie is wearing a black dress. 

If you have problems with vocabulary during the activity, use the online dictionary. You will see it at the end of each section.
