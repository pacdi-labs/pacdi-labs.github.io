Conjugation - Present Simple

Now that you comprehend the definitions, we can start with conjugations. The conjugation of a verb is to adapt it depending on the tense (past, present, or future). 

In Spanish we have conjugations too.

For example "jugar" is the verb and "juego / juegas / juega / jugamos / juegan" are the conjugations for present tense.

The dynamic for conjugation in English is similar, study the information in the PDF below to identify the changes necessary to conjugate the verbs in Present Simple.
