Question

Which of these options defines the term "verb"? Select the correct answer.

Answers

A verb is used to describe a subject. 

A verb is used to express actions. 

A verb is used to represent a person, animal, or thing.

Feedback

Nop. Terms that describe subjects are adjectives! 

Yes! A verb is an action! 

Noo, subjects represent a person, animals, or things.

Solution

  1. Wrong (Feedback)
  2. Correct Option (Feedback)
  3. Wrong (Feedback)

Find

The correct position of the verb. Select and move the words into the correct order to create sentences. 

Identify the verb and move it to the correct position, be fast... there is a time limit!
