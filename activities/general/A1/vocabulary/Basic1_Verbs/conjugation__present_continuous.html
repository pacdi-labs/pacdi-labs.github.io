Conjugation - Present Continuous

Present Simple is considered one of the most basic tenses to learn when you study English. Another simple but important tense is Present Continuous. 

The equivalent in Spanish is the suffix "-ando"

For example "jugar" is the verb and "jugando" is the conjugation for present continuous.

The dynamic for the conjugation of this tense is different to Present Simple, study the information in the PDF below to compare the elements necessary to construct Present Simple and Present Continuous.
