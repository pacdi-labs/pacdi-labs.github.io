Types of crimes and criminals

Crimes and criminals

In English, there are a lot of specific words for different types of crimes and the criminals who commit them. Unfortunately, the list of crimes and criminals is long because the words have specific legal meanings.

Here is a list of the most common crimes and who commits them. In case that you need a clearer definition, you can consult the dictionary. 

CRIME DEFINITION  CRIMINAL
murder killing someone on purpose murderer
kidnapping taking people away by force and demands money to free them kidnapper
burglary to enter a building, often while no one
is in it, and steal money or objects		 burglar
mugging to take something by force
from someone, often in the
street		 mugger
pickpocketing to steal from someone’s pocket
etc, without them realizing		 pickpocket
arson setting fire to a building, cars or property on purpose arsonist
rape forcing someone to have sex rapist
hijacking to use force to take control of a plane, ship, train, etc. hijacker
fraud to deceive or cheat
someone to get money		  -
theft stealing, in general, usually without violence thief
smuggling taking things secretly in or out of a place like drugs, etc. smuggler
shoplifting stealing something from a store shoplifter
manslaughter killing someone by accident   -
blackmail threatening to reveal someone’s secrets if a lot of money is not paid blackmailer
bribery giving money or granting favors to influence another person’s decisions or behavior briber
spying secretly gets information about another country spy
robbery stealing large amounts of money with force or violence from a bank, store, etc. robber
