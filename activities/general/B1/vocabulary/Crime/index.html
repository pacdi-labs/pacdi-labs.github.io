Introduction

Do you feel safe?

 

Crime is one of the most major problems not only in Mexico but around the world. Some people do not work to obtain the things they want so they think it is easier to take them from others. No one has the right to take anyway anything from another person that does not belong to them, it is supposed to be a society norm,  however, crimes do not stop. Every day in the newspapers or in the TV news, crimes and criminals are a trending topic. 

Before we move on, answer these questions in your portfolio.

1. Have your ever been the victim of a crime? If so, explain thse situation.

2. Are some parts of this city considered more dangerous than others? Which parts?

3. Do you think there is a link between drugs and crime?

4. What are some things people can do to protect themselves from crime?

5. Why do people commit crimes?
