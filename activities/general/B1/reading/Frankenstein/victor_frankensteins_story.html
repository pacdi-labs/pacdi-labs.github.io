Victor Frankenstein's Story

More than words

To obtain a higher comprehension of literary or academic readings, it is important to possess an extensive knowledge of vocabulary.

Before reading, search the definition of the terms below to encourage a higher comprehension of Frankenstein's Chapter 1.    

  • captain 
  • mast
  • huge
  • through
  • sledge
  • round
  • sailor
  • enough
  • faint (fainted)
  • to be pleased
  • disappear (disappeared)
You may use a physical dictionary or click on "Cambridge Dictionary"  to search for the meanings online. 

Frankenstein by Mary Shelley

Now that you know some of the words that you will find in the text, read Chapter 1.

If you come across any new terms, look them up in a dictionary too!

First catch

In maximum 10 sentences, summarize what you read in Chapter 1.
