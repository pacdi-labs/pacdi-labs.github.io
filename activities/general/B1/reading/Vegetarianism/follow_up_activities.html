Follow up activities

Activity

Now that you have informed yourself about vegetarianism, you will watch part of an episode of the American animated television serie "The Simpsons".  Once you finish watching it, reflect and answer the following questions in your notebook. 

Before you watch it!

What has happened before the scene you are about to watch?

The Simpson family visits a petting zoo, where Lisa is enraptured by a cute lamb. That night when the family is having dinner (they are eating lamb chops), Lisa is troubled by the connection between the dish and the living creature, and announces that she will not longer eat meat. Her brother Bart and her father Homer mock her about her new ideals, as well as her classmates later on. 

At school, Lisa requests a vegetarian alternative to the cafeteria food and she gets a piece of bread. The school principal forces students to watch a propaganda film which criticizes vegetarianism.

Homer and Bart continue to give Lisa a hard time, particularly since Homer is ready to host a barbecue.

Go ahead and watch the video...

                                                                

Time to reflect!

Answer the following questions in your notebook.

1. What is your opinion towards Lisa's classmates teasing her?

2. Would you make fun of a relative or a classmate because he/she is vegetarian? Why?

3. How do you think Lisa feels in Homer's barbecue when she offers an option of food instead of meat? Why?

4. What do you think about Lisa's final reaction (when she pushes the pig)?

5. Can Lisa force Homer's guests to eat gazpacho or can Homer force Lisa to eat meat? Why?
