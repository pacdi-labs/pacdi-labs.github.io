    How self-sufficient are you? 

    Think about the activities others do for you, the permissions you ask or give, the things you are forced to do or the things you force others to do. Write 10 sentences in your notebook using the causative verbs, try to use different grammatical tenses to practice. Remember to respect the rules in every tense. 

    Example: I have had my house decorated last month.  (present perfect)
