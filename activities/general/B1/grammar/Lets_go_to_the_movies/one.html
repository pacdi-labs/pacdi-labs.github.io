How much do you know about movies? Let's find out...

After reading the text, complete the activities with the information you just read.

Movie fans come in all different types as so the movies that they enjoy.  Films have covered every aspects of the human experience from showing serious struggles to reflecting history and also just making us laugh or even scream.  Like with all art forms, movies mirror human life and also influence it in many ways. Movies can be divided into several different genres. 

Action movies are usually about a conflict between a hero or a group of heroes and one or more villains. Movies like these almost always reach a climax near the end in which the hero and the villain meet for a final battle. This climax usually includes some of the movie's most spectacular scenes and special effects. Not all action movies feature heroes and villains. Some feature anti-heroes who face terrible dangers and will do whatever they have to do to survive. However, not all these films are ralated to super-heroes, there are others about high-speed car chases, martial arts, gun battles and big explosions.

Not everyone loves action movies, some people prefer to go to the cinema to watch serial killers, ghosts and monsters. Many early horror movies were based on old stories about scary creatures like vampires who terrify their victims by biting their necks and drinking their blood. Films about supernatural beings like ghosts and spirits, and movies about spooky places like haunted houses can be very scary as well. These movies use creepy sound effects and music that frighten even brave people. 

Since the early days of cinema, comedy has been one of its most popular and successful genres. A good comedy has lots of scenes with funny characters or funny situations that make us laugh.Characters can be funny if they're clever and say or do witty  things, or if they're socially inept and say or do embarrassing things, or if they're not very clever and they say and do stupid things. Scenes can be funny if something unexpected or shocking happens or if something embarrassing or ridiculous happens. Films that find humour in these and other serious topics like war, illness and death are often called black comedies. Movies that feature very strange or very crazy humour are often called absurd comedies. Another popular kind of comedy is the road movie in which characters have a series of funny adventures while travelling together. 

Comedy and drama were the earliest genres of cinema, and they're still the most popular genres today. Dramas entertain us by telling interesting stories. A good drama does more than just tell a good story, however. It makes us care about the characters and feel many emotions. If something good happens we feel like smiling, if something sad happens we feel like crying, and if something bad is done to a character we like we might even feel angry. If we feel emotions like these we'll get involved in the story and find it more entertaining. But dramas can do even more than this. They can also make us think about important issues and teach us important lessons about life and how to live. There are many different genres of drama and some of the most popular are the mystery, police drama, political drama, biopics that are about real people and docudramas about real events are also very popular. The variety is extense. 

There are other types of movies that are popular. If you love watching movies about the future or movies set in outer space, you're a fan of science fiction or "sci-fi". We might see what living in a high-tech future world could be like. We might see people travelling in time, or androids that look like human beings, or huge starships that travel to distant galaxies. But not just that, there are also movies that recall or are based on historical events in the past; or for those who are learning about the real world and real people probably like watching documentaries. 

After watching a movie, you might want to ask questions like:

  • "Which character did you like the best?"
  • "What did you think of the plot?"
  • "Did you like the cast?"
  • "What was your favourite scene?

It does not matter your personality or the things you are into, there is always going to be a film that will captivate you. If you have not find a favorite genre yet, keep watching movies!
