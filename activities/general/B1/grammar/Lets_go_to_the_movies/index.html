Three...

 

Movies have become very popular in the last century. It is the favorite pastime of many people across the world. The best thing about movies is that there are different genres. This means that everyone can find something interesting. Even though many movies are produced every year, there are some that are more popular than others. Movies have a part in influencing the masses and so are a great way of spreading information about things that affect the world.

  • Answer these questions in your notebook.

1. What type of movies do you like to watch?

2. Do you prefer watching films at the cinema or at home?

3. Do you have a favorite movie? If that is the case, which one is it and why? 

4. Who do you think plays the best role in a film making, the actors, the directors, the producers, or the screeenwriter? Why? 

Before you continue...                                                                           

This activity is destined for English IV, if you have a lower level it might be difficult for you to comprehend and answer some activities. 

In order to answer this activity, you need to have knowledge about the grammatical tenses (past, present and future) and their rules; also you need to know the difference between active and passive voice..
