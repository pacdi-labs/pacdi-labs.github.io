Conclusion

Put the ideas in order to complete the conclusion.

  • Now you know what relative clauses are.
  • You can use them whenever you want to describe something, someone
  • or whenever you want to tell extra information about something or someone.
  • Don't forget to use commas (or make a pause if you are speaking)
  • if you are giving extra information.
  • Also, never use that when you give extra information.
