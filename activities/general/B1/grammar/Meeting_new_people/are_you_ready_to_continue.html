Are you ready to continue?

Objectives

In this material, you will learn about relative clauses and the correct uses of relative pronouns. 

You will identify the difference between  restrictive and non-restrictive relative clauses. 

This activity is for students in B1 level.

 

Before we move on!

Observe the image, after that, complete the followiong exercises.

Do you know it?

1. Complete the sentences with the name of the subject according to the information.

1. JXUwMDFjJXUwMDI1JXUwMDEzJXUwMDFlJXUwMDBk is the girl who is holding the lamp.

2. JXUwMDFhJXUwMDMwJXUwMDA3JXUwMDE2JXUwMDA2 is the person who is eating all the cake. 

3. JXUwMDEyJXUwMDI1JXUwMDA3JXUwMDA2 is the guy whose belongings are being used by someone else. 

4. JXUwMDFmJXUwMDJlJXUwMDA1 is a dog whose owner is JXUwMDBjJXUwMDNiJXUwMDAxJXUwMDE3 .

5. JXUwMDE5JXUwMDIwJXUwMDEzJXUwMDFkJXUwMDAx is a boy that is pulling JXUwMDE5JXUwMDJmJXUwMDBm 's hair.  

2. What are the parts of the sentences? 

Identify the subject, the verb and the object in the following sentences. 

Write subject, verb or object in the blank space depending on the part of the sentence to which the word is describing. 

1. He carries the dog.

The dog is the JXUwMDM3JXUwMDBkJXUwMDA4JXUwMDBmJXUwMDA2JXUwMDE3 .

2. He ate the whole cake. 

Cake is the JXUwMDM3JXUwMDBkJXUwMDA4JXUwMDBmJXUwMDA2JXUwMDE3 . 

He is the JXUwMDJiJXUwMDA2JXUwMDE3JXUwMDA4JXUwMDBmJXUwMDA2JXUwMDE3 . 

Ate is the JXUwMDJlJXUwMDEzJXUwMDE3JXUwMDEw . 

3. The boy bothers the girl.

The girl is the JXUwMDM3JXUwMDBkJXUwMDA4JXUwMDBmJXUwMDA2JXUwMDE3 .

The boy is the JXUwMDJiJXUwMDA2JXUwMDE3JXUwMDA4JXUwMDBmJXUwMDA2JXUwMDE3 .
