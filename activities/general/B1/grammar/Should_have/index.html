I should've studied English before

Hello and welcome!

This is a grammar activity corresponding to B1 level.  

At this point of your life, you know how to express ideas in present, past and future.

  But... what about the things that never happened? Or the things that could have been different? Today you're going to learn a new way to say very useful things.

  Let's start!
