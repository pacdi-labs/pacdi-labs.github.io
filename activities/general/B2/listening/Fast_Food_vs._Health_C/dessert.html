Dessert

Reflection

Saying no to delicious but unhealthy food can be difficult! Even so, an abuse will lead to dangerous consequences.

Watch the following video and in your notebook express what you think about the protagonist's situation. Use these questions to guide you: 

➡ Which could be possible reasons for her condition?

➡ How could it have gotten to that point?

➡ Which consequences does she now have to deal with?

➡ What would you recommend to her? 

✳ Use facts from the Duran's audio to support your arguments.
