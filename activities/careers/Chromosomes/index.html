Why am I freckled?

Between 1856 and 1863, some experiments revealed how traits are passed down from parents, then studies presented in 1865, showed that what we now call “genes” determine traits in predictable ways.

Activity goal

After doing this activity students will be able to understand better what chromosomes are, how they are formed and their function in the human body.
