Urinary tract?

The urinary tract is divided into:

  • Upper urinary tract:  kidneys and ureters
  • Low urinary tract:  urinary bladder and urethra

Let's see if you can identify these organs. Do the following activity.

Urinary Tract
Match the parts of the urinary tract with its name.

kidney

ureter

bladder

urethra

upper urinary tract

low urinary tract

In this session we are focusing on the upper urinary tract and the suprarenal glands.
