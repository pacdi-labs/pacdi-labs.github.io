Objectives

What will you learn?

In this activity you will learn concepts related to skeletal muscle. You will read information about various aspects of skeletal muscle tissue including its development, structure, function and morphology.

This activity is for students in A2 level.

Before we move on!

In this material you will find medical vocabulary. If you are not familiar with medical terms, some of the activities in the next sections will be difficult for you to understrand. Try to complete the following activity, if you don't understand any of the terms, you will need to choose a different material that corresponds to your level.   

Let's see if this material is for you! 

                                   

Was the previous activity too difficult for you to understand? If it wasn't too difficult for you, and you understand some of the terms, you can continue working with this material. If you don't understand the terms, look for another activity.
